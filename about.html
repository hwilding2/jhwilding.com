our site



jhwilding.com

JHWilding.com is a hub for family history as it happens. I wanted a central place where we could keep track of plans and goals (both met and unmet), memories of our life together, and moments we captured on camera. I wanted to be able to see our story and character arcs, pardon the analogy, as they develop. I wanted a place where I could look back and see God's hand in our lives. This life is what we make of it, and by reflecting on and recording our life, I hope to be inspired to make it a good one. JHWilding.com offers a birds eye view of what is contained in our personal journals. Welcome to the rollercoaster of our lives! Perhaps there's something here that speaks to challenges you're facing, or experiences you've had, and that you can find some solidarity or inspiration through your time here!

up-and-coming

Like our lives, JHWilding is constantly under construction. Up-and-coming functionality includes: a blog post creation, storage, and filtering system; a personalized about page for each member of the Wilding family; and a photo gallery of our personal library--books we've read over the course of our time together.
